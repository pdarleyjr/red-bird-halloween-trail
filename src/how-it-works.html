How It Works

🎃 Family-Friendly

Our trail is designed for all ages with gentle scares and generous treats. Perfect for young children and families exploring together.

🛡️ Safety First

Walk during daylight hours and stay on designated paths. Parents accompany young children, and everyone respects personal space.

🚗 Carpool When Possible

Park at designated areas and walk between houses. This reduces traffic and makes the experience more enjoyable for everyone.

🤝 Be Kind & Tidy

Respect our neighbors' property and keep the trail clean. Say thank you and enjoy the festive atmosphere with good spirits.

🗺️ Follow the Icons

Look for our trail markers and house signs. The interactive map shows all participating locations with their unique themes.

📸 Share the Fun

Take photos of your favorite decorations and share them with #RedBirdTrail. Help spread the Halloween spirit in our community!
